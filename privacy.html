Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

Stratify ("the app") is a stock screening tool for individual investors and traders. This privacy policy explains how we handle your information.

Information We Collect

Stratify does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information. The app displays publicly available stock market data fetched from financial data providers.

Data Storage

Any data you save within the app (such as your watchlist or notes) is stored locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

Third-Party Services

The app displays stock charts and data from TradingView and Yahoo Finance. These services have their own privacy policies which govern their data collection practices.

Market Data

Stock prices, financial data, and market information displayed in the app are sourced from public financial data providers for informational purposes only. This is not financial advice.

No Account Required

Stratify does not require you to create an account or provide any personal information to use the app.

Children's Privacy

Stratify is intended for users 18 years and older. We do not knowingly collect information from anyone under 18.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

Contact
